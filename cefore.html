About the Game

Did you ever want to be an expert at blowing stuff up? Cefore is a physics-based puzzler with a whimsical and simplistic art style. You play as Baro, an intergalactic contractor in charge of gathering data on different planets. To do so, Baro uses tools—explosive charges, grapples, hot air balloons, and more—to destroy the environment and gather what he needs.

As structures get bigger and more complex, your demolition methods must get more creative. But try not to cause too much destruction!

Features

  • Visit different planets — Play through different biomes and themes
  • 100% destructible environment — Everything is affected by physics
  • Level Editor — Create, share, and play levels with Steam Workshop
  • Game modes — Missions, level editor, and sandbox
  • Multiple tools — Explosives, grapples, balloons, and more
  • Character customization — Unlock skins and hats for Baro
  • Jet-pack — Fly around the environment
  • Over 25 unique levels
