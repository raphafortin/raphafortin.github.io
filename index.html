The Studio

Pixelz Games was founded in 2014 by Raphael Fortin and a handful of graduate students who worked together on CO-OP: Decrypted. Based in Montreal, we’ve been making games that are fun and cozy ever since—from robot co-op puzzles to physics-based demolition to, well, running a virtual café.

We don’t take ourselves too seriously. We just like building things that feel good to play. Barista: Rise and Grind is currently in the oven, developed by Raphael (CEO and pretty much everything else on this one).
