About the Game

In Barista: Rise and Grind, you play as Bean, a rookie barista whose dream is to open their own café. You start your journey at a small coffee stand on the street with low quality equipment and work to realize your dream of owning your very own successful café.

BRaG is a top-view cozy simulation game where you manage all aspects of your café—from sourcing beans and roasting to customizing your space and serving customers.

Features

  • Character creation — Customize your Bean from head to toe with hats, glasses, aprons, and accessories
  • Buying the greens — Source coffee beans by origin, varietal, process, and altitude
  • Roasting — Roast green beans to increase profit and control drink quality
  • Upgrading spaces — Purchase bigger properties and new equipment to unlock recipes
  • Your Café — Customize music, seating, workspace layout, decor, and more
  • Your Home — Personal apartment to plan, restock, and rest
