CO-OP: Decrypted

A 2.5D puzzle-platformer. Control two robots—R3D, who absorbs light, and BLU3, who converts it into explosive power—as they escape a recycling facility under drone surveillance.

Buy on Steam — $9.99

About the Game

R3D is activated in what appears to be a recycling facility. He is subjected to functionality tests under the surveillance of the factory's drones. While following their instructions, his receptors connect with BLU3's—another robot that seems to share R3D's unwilling fate.

Together, they must cooperate to free themselves from their captors and escape from the "recycling" facility.

Features

  • Local co-op — Play with a friend on the same screen
  • Two robots with unique powers — R3D absorbs light; BLU3 converts it to explosive power
  • Full dynamic lighting — Physics-based light absorption and interaction
  • Steam achievements — 20 achievements to unlock
  • Unlockable content — Discover extras as you progress
  • Difficulty levels — Different settings change gameplay elements
  • Original soundtrack
  • Cross-platform multiplayer — Remote Play Together supported
